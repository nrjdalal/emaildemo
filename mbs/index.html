WHY BAMBOO?
The Brilliant Solution To Sustainability
As a natural resource, bamboo offers a myriad of advantages that make it the best solution to reducing waste and creating sustainable solutions to stubborn business problems.
RE-USABLE
Bamboo extends the life of each straw by 100 times! Save money and reduce waste one straw at a time.
BIODEGRADABLE
When you eventually get rid of your bamboo straws, you don’t have to worry about whether it will contribute to pollution because, as a natural product, it will decay on its own without harming the environment.
SUSTAINABLE
In making products that are 100% natural, recyclable, biodegradable, and re- usable, we are serving the future by creating a sustainable way of life. A sustainable approach means you reduce waste and effectively manage natural resources for today’s and tomorrow’s generations. We even use sustainable product packaging.
ZERO WASTE
Waste is a thing of the past with our premium re-usable bamboo drinking straws. This is the first step towards creating resource life cycles where all products can be re-used. At Mr. Bamboo Straws, our mission is to stop sending trash to the ocean, incinerators, or landfills.
100% NATURAL
We really mean it. There are no chemicals or pesticides used at any stage of the process – during growth, harvest, or production. Completely natural and entirely harmless to the environment.
ECO-FRIENDLY
Bamboo straws can’t damage the environment because whether you have them recycled or simply throw them away, they don’t contain harmful chemicals or pesticides. This means they will either naturally decay, or you can have them recycled to create new straws.
HANDMADE WITH LOVE
Each straw is harvested from government-controlled national parks in Vietnam. This ensures that only the excess bamboo is used for straws, and the factory where they are manufactured makes sure not to use any chemicals during the process. We can also brand your straws using the best quality laser-marking.

DIMENSIONS
Size Us Up
The standard length of our bamboo straws is 20cm or 12,5cm. But you can also request other lengths and we will happily deliver a customized product that meets your exact specifications.
HOTEL RESORTS
PREMIUM GIVE-AWAYS
Get Bamboo Straws in these dimensions
For normal high-ball glasses, you will need the 20cm straws, and for low-ball or whiskey glasses and cocktail glasses, you will need 12,5cm straws. The diameter will vary thanks to the natural thickness of each individual bamboo piece, but we divide diameters for different drinks into 5mm-11mm for normal drinks and 11mm-15mm for thick smoothies, bubble tea and milkshakes.
PACKAGING
Choose Standard Or Personalized Packaging
For our standard packaging, your bamboo straws are 20cm or 12,5cm long which varies slightly based on the bamboo thickness. We separate the diameters into 5mm-11mm for normal straws and 11mm-15mm for thicker drinks like smoothies, bubble tea and milkshakes. We use sustainable product packaging as it’s made of recycled materials – the cardboard boxes come from recycled Kraft paper to make it as eco-friendly as possible. Our pouches carry 2 or 5 bamboo straws, while our cardboard boxes carry 25, 50, 100, 200, or 1000 straws.
Personalized Packaging
We are also happy to create unique packaging that captures your specific brand and style. Simply send us the logo and preferred text, and we will insert this on all our sustainable product packaging.
